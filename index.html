Who We Are

Dedicated to Elder Dignity

Devashobha Sanstha Trust is Mumbai's premier non-profit organization committed to providing world-class care for elderly citizens who need it most.

Premium Accommodation

30-bed facility with modern amenities, ensuring comfort and safety for all residents in a peaceful Mumbai location.

Comprehensive Healthcare

Round-the-clock medical support with regular doctor visits, emergency services, and medicine management.

Nutritious Meals

Home-style cooking with three daily meals plus evening tea and snacks, prepared with love and nutrition in mind.

Social Activities

Regular cultural programs, festivals, group activities, and digital literacy programs to keep residents engaged.

Legal Support

Advocacy for senior citizen rights and legal assistance to ensure our residents' interests are protected.

Emotional Wellbeing

Professional counseling services and a nurturing environment that treats every resident as family.
